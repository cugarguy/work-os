## Executive Summary

**EDS Portfolio:** DataSync, Transfer Family (includes B2Bi), and Storage Gateway
- **Team Size:** 121 total (111 builders, 10 PM)
- **YTD Revenue:** $213.6M (+12.7% YoY, +0.3% vs. OP2)
- **2026 Target:** $279M (+18.3% YoY)

## Key Business Challenges

1. **Storage Gateway declining revenue:** $24.7M YTD (-7.5% YoY)
2. **Transfer Family slowing growth:** +15% in 2025 (vs +26% in 2023, +17% in 2024)
3. **DataSync pricing model:** Basic mode below cost for some workloads

## Strategic Shift: Storage Gateway

**Major Change:** SGW moving to KTLO (Keep The Lights On) mode
- **Phoenix project paused:** Single site NAS replacement offering cancelled
- **Resource reallocation:** Shifting resources to MFT and platform initiatives
- **Rationale:** Demand signals for hybrid storage not as strong as MFT demand

## 2026 Strategy Focus

### Two Primary Areas:
1. **MFT Market Expansion** ($3-4B market, 10% CAGR)
2. **Operational Efficiency** through "Tenon" platform

### Key Metrics to Track:
- Transfer Family revenue from customers using >SFTP endpoints (currently 60%)
- DataSync revenue from recurring transfers vs migrations (currently 29%)

## Service Performance YTD

### DataSync
- **Revenue:** $10.7M (+74.8% YoY)
- **Enhanced Mode Success:** 31% of service revenue (up from 17% Q1)
- **ASP Increase:** $0.015/GB (+33% YoY)

### Transfer Family & B2Bi
- **Revenue:** $178.5M (+14% YoY)
- **SFTP dominance:** 98% of revenue ($203M ARR)
- **MFT customers:** 12% of base, 60% of ARR, growing 28% YoY

### Storage Gateway
- **Revenue:** $24.1M (-8.3% YoY)
- **Status:** KTLO mode, minimal HC investment
- **Customer decline:** -14% YoY

## Major 2026 Initiatives

### Tenon Platform
- **Purpose:** Unified on-premises appliance platform
- **Benefits:** Reduce operational burden across DataSync and SGW teams
- **Timeline:** MVP for AWS Backup RipTide launch, SGW/DataSync support in 2027

### MFT Capabilities Expansion
- **DataSync:** Enhanced mode rollout completion, recurring transfer focus
- **Transfer Family:** Beyond SFTP capabilities (web apps, connectors, AS2/B2Bi)

### Enterprise Features
- Multi-region SFTP endpoints
- Private cross-cloud connectivity
- Fine-grained access controls

## Regional Expansion
