# MBR Research - December 2025

## MFT Market Analysis

### Market Size and Growth
- **Total Market:** $3-4B
- **Growth Rate:** 10% CAGR
- **Strategic Priority:** Primary focus area for EDS 2026 strategy

### Customer Segments

#### Enterprise MFT Customers
- **Percentage of Base:** 12% of Transfer Family customers
- **Revenue Contribution:** 60% of ARR ($123M)
- **Growth Rate:** 28% YoY
- **Characteristics:** >10 SFTP endpoints, adopt broader product portfolio

#### Key Enterprise Customers
- **Financial Services:** JPMC, Capital One, Vanguard, Goldman Sachs, FINRA
- **Healthcare:** Healthfirst, Abbvie, Quidel Ortho
- **Manufacturing/Logistics:** Thomson Reuters, Eli Lily, Haliburton, Geodis
- **Technology:** Netflix, Disney, Meta, Anthropic, Salesforce

## MFT Requirements Analysis

### Core Capabilities Needed
1. **Beyond SFTP:** Web apps, connectors, AS2, B2Bi (EDI)
2. **Enterprise Features:** Multi-region replication, private connectivity
3. **Workflow Integration:** Event-driven transfers, audit logs
4. **Scalability:** Support for 1000s of transfer jobs
5. **Security:** Fine-grained access controls, compliance certifications

### Current Gaps
- **SFTP Dependency:** 98% of Transfer Family revenue from SFTP endpoints
- **Limited Adoption:** MFT features taking longer than expected for enterprise adoption
- **Integration Complexity:** Need for unified customer experience across services

## Competitive Landscape

### Traditional MFT Providers
- **Legacy Solutions:** Customers eager to migrate from legacy MFT solutions
- **Use Cases:** Financial reconciliation, video distribution, lab data pipelines, AI/ML training
- **Volume:** Millions of file transfers per month for critical business workflows

### AWS Positioning
- **Advantage:** Cloud-native, integrated with AWS services
- **Challenge:** Feature parity with established MFT providers
- **Opportunity:** Unified experience across DataSync and Transfer Family

## Revenue Opportunity

### Current Performance
- **Transfer Family:** $178.5M YTD (+14% YoY)
- **DataSync:** $10.7M YTD (+74.8% YoY)
- **Combined Growth:** Driven by Enhanced mode and MFT adoption

### 2026+ Projections
- **Target:** Sustained 20% YoY growth for EDS
- **Driver:** Increased MFT market penetration
- **Metric:** Growth in customers using >SFTP endpoints

## Strategic Initiatives

### Product Development
1. **DataSync MFT Features:** Recurring transfers, event-driven architecture
2. **Transfer Family Expansion:** Web apps, AS2 enhancements, B2Bi growth
3. **Unified Experience:** Common platform for file transfer workflows

### Market Expansion
1. **Vertical Focus:** M&E, Life Sciences, FinServ
2. **Geographic Expansion:** EMEA and LATAM support (EDIFACT format)
3. **Enterprise Sales:** Target >$200K customers with comprehensive MFT needs

## Success Metrics

### Revenue Metrics
- **Transfer Family:** % revenue from customers using >SFTP endpoints (target: increase from 60%)
- **DataSync:** % revenue from recurring transfers vs migrations (target: increase from 29%)
- **Deal Size:** Average MFT customer ARR (target: >$200K for enterprise)

### Adoption Metrics
- **Feature Utilization:** Web apps, AS2, B2Bi adoption rates
- **Customer Expansion:** Existing customers adopting additional MFT capabilities
- **Market Share:** Position relative to traditional MFT providers
